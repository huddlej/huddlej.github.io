Remember

  • We coexist with viruses.
  • We have the power to change viruses as much as they change us.
  • What we know about viruses depends on government funding, public health labs, and academic research.
  • When you put on a raincoat, you protect yourself.
    When you get vaccinated, you protect yourself and others.
